About Me

I am a dynamic frontend developer with a passion for crafting engaging, user-friendly web experiences. I specialize in building responsive and interactive interfaces using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, and Bootstrap, with additional experience in Next.js and SaaS. My programming foundation includes JavaScript, Python, C++, and Node.js, while my backend knowledge spans Firebase, Supabase, MongoDB, and SQL. I am well-versed in version control systems like Git and GitHub, and I leverage frameworks such as FastAPI and Express.js to streamline development workflows. I work comfortably across various development environments including Visual Studio Code, Code::Blocks, Jupyter Notebook, and Ubuntu. Beyond coding, I have a strong eye for design and proficiency in graphic tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, XD, Figma, InDesign, CorelDRAW, and Canva. I thrive in collaborative environments where creativity and innovation flourish, and I am constantly exploring new technologies, attending tech meetups, and sharing knowledge with the developer community. With a drive for continuous learning and a commitment to excellence, I strive to deliver high-quality solutions and make a lasting impact in the world of frontend development.
